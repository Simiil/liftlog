# Privacy Policy — LiftLog

**Last updated: 11 June 2026**

LiftLog is an offline workout tracker. **your data never
leaves your device.** The app has no accounts, no servers, and no network
access at all.

## Data the app stores

LiftLog stores the data you enter while using it:

- Workout logs: exercises, sets, weights, repetitions, and timestamps
- Training plans you create
- App settings (such as weight unit, language, and theme)

All of this is kept in the app's private storage on your device. It is never
transmitted anywhere by the app.

## What the app does *not* do

- **No network access.** The app does not request the internet permission, so
  it is technically incapable of sending data off your device.
- **No accounts or registration.** There is nothing to sign up for.
- **No analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash reporting.**
- **No third-party services or SDKs** that receive your data.
- **No permissions.** The app requests no Android permissions whatsoever.

## Export files

You can export your training data to a file at any time. Exports happen only
when you initiate them, and the file is saved or shared wherever **you**
choose. Once exported, that file is under your control — anyone you share it
with can read it.

## Device backups

If you have Android's device backup enabled, the operating system may include
LiftLog's data in your device backup (for example, in your Google account) and
in device-to-device transfers when you move to a new phone. This is an
operating-system feature controlled by you in your device's system settings —
the app itself transmits nothing. Such backups are governed by your device or
Google account terms, not by LiftLog.

## Data deletion

Uninstalling the app, or clearing its storage in Android's settings,
permanently deletes all data the app stores on your device. Export files you
created and OS backups remain wherever you (or your device's backup settings)
put them.

## Children

LiftLog collects no data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes in a way that affects this policy, the updated policy
will be published at this same location with a new date at the top.
